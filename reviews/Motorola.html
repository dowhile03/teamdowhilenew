Motorola Fusion Plus Review

By Hrishabh Singhal

SPECIFICATIONS


• Body: 162.9x76.4x9.6mm, 210g; Gorilla Glass front, plastic frame and back. Splash-resistant.
• Screen: 6.5" IPS LCD, 1080x2340px resolution, 19.5:9 aspect ratio, 385ppi; no cutout, HDR10.
• Chipset: Snapdragon 730 (8nm): Octa-core CPU (2x2.2 GHz Kryo 470 Gold & 6x1.8 GHz Kryo 470 Silver); Adreno 618 GPU.
• Memory: 6GB RAM, 128GB built-in UFS 2.1 storage, (shared) microSD slot.
• OS/Software: Android 10.
• Rear camera: Wide (main): 64MP, Quad-Bayer, 1/1.72" sensor, 0.8µm pixel size, 26mm equiv. focal length, f/1.8 aperture, PDAF. Ultrawide: 8MP, 1/4.0", 1.12µm, 13mm, f/2.2, AF. Macro: 5MP, f/2.2. Depth: 2MP, f/2.2. Video recording: up to 2160p/30fps.
• Front camera: 16MP, Quad-Bayer, f/2.0, 1.0µm, fixed focus. 1080p/30fps video recording.
• Battery: 5,000mAh, 15W wired charging support
• Misc: Rear-mounted fingerprint reader; FM radio; 3.5mm headphone jack.

REVIEW

Motorola is back with its new mid range budget oriented phone fusion plus. This device is definitely one of the best available deals in this price section.
The best thing that I liked about this device is its stock android feature. Very few mobile phones provide this feature is this price except MI android lineup, but than you know its Mi(pun intended). Stock android gives a whole new user experience, with its add free services and zero bloatware and all google services.
When it comes to display it comes with a 6.5 inch LCD panel which HDR 10 certified. It has a bezzelless display with a slight chin at the bottom without a punch hole or dew drop. This display also supports DCI-P3 for 25% more colors and more vibrant look.
When it comes to performance it is packed with snapdragon 730G processor and 6GB of ram which provides it with good amount of power. The UI of the device is designed in a way to get most out of the software. It a stock android device with slight of that Motorola touch. This processor can handle any kind of functionality even those PUBG matches without any delay(XD). Though it does not supports 5G.
This phone also comes with impressive camera performance. The main one is a 64-megapixel Samsung sensor with F1.8 aperture lens, which is accompanied by a combination of an 8-megapixel ultra-wide camera with a 118-degree FOV, a 5-megapixel macro camera and a 2-megapixel depth camera. The front 16-megapixel camera sits in the pop-up mechanism. This pop-up module isn’t the fastest one and it takes a full second to come out. The camera on the whole are amazing.
Ultimately, this is a perfect device if you are looking for a mobile phone for power with best user experience and fresh updates.

POINTS TO HAVE A LOOK
  1. This phone is made of plastic body, maybe many of don’t appreciate that much in 2020.
  2. This also a dated camera setup, that same four rings on the left side placed vertically.

It is available for sale at 16,999 Indian rupees
